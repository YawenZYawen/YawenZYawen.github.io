Short bio

I am currently a first second third fourth year Ph.D. student at the College of Information Science and Technology, Pennsylvania State University, supervised by Prof. Xiang Zhang. I obtained my B.Eng degree in computer science and technology from Beihang University in 2017, supervised by Prof. Shuai Ma. I was an exchange student at Technical University of Munich, Munich, Germany. My research focuses on graph mining and graph related deep learning.
